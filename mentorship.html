From foundation to live capital — one trader at a time

Program Structure
3 Phases · 12 Months
Phase 1
Foundation
Months 1–3
Learn how the market actually works. Six modules with built-in 90-question quiz coverage. Sequential unlocks — no skipping.
Markets, charts, small-caps, platforms, risk, psychology
Trade-Ideas scanner setup, Sterling Trader Pro, IB TWS
Hotkey drills until execution is reflex

Phase 2
Paper Trading
Months 4–9
$30,000 paper account. Six months of live drilling under real session conditions until the habits that make money are automatic.
10 daily drills — thesis, setup ID, bracket speed, journaling
5 setup playbooks — Gap-and-Go, VWAP Reclaim, PMH Rejection, Gap-Fill, 10:30 Rule
8-metric performance dashboard with Firebase tracking
Phase Gate: must hit 8 criteria to advance
Phase 3
Live Capital
Months 10–12
Real money. Tightened rules. 12-week supervised ramp from micro size to full size with daily mentor check-ins, tapering to weekly by graduation.
12-week ramp — Micro → 25% → 50% → 100% size
Daily 2% / weekly 5% loss limits, max 1% per trade
Crisis & Recovery playbook — first loss, tilt, 3-loss rule
Mentor tapering: M10 daily → M11 2x/wk → M12 1x/wk + exit interview

⚠ Honest Expectations
Day trading is hard. Most people who attempt it lose money — that statistic is real and not excluded by signing up here. This program is designed to give serious traders a structured shot at consistency, not a guaranteed outcome. The 12-month structure exists because building professional trading habits takes time. Phase 3 requires its own real capital separate from tuition. If those facts deter you, this isn't your program — and that's OK.
