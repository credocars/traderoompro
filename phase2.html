Months 4–9 · Paper Trading with Live Mentor Sessions
Welcome to Phase 2
Foundation gave you the rules. Phase 2 is where they become reflex. For the next six months you trade paper — every morning, with the mentor, with full accountability — until the habits that make money possible are automatic. The goal is not P&L. The goal is to graduate to Phase 3 ready.

How drills work
Each drill builds a specific habit. Click a drill to expand instructions, scoring rubric, and mentor sign-off requirements. Click "Mark today's session complete" after performing the drill to log it. Mentor reviews and grades — the log shows your completion streak.
Five tradeable setups
Each playbook covers catalyst requirements, entry trigger, stop placement, target rules, and the disqualifiers that mean SKIP. During Months 6–7, you will pick two of these to specialize in.
Track Your Numbers Manually
Phase 2 dashboard is intentionally manual — the act of typing your weekly stats forces you to look at them. Pull data from TraderVue every Friday after market close, enter the values below, and review trends with your mentor. The weekly entries save automatically and build a history you can review during monthly self-review.
Graduation requirements
Eight criteria stand between you and Phase 3 (live capital). All must be satisfied AND the mentor must approve. Numerical criteria are necessary but not sufficient — the final decision is the mentor's after a 60-minute exit interview.
From anecdotal to statistical
Most traders rely on intuition: "I think gap-and-go works." Top traders rely on data: "Gap-and-go on float under 10M with PMH break has 64% follow-through over my last 200 samples." This tab teaches you to build that database using Polygon.io API. Activates Months 6-7 specialization with real numbers, not feelings.
📋
The 14-Column Schema
Every trade you take, AND every setup you skipped, gets one row in your database. The schema below covers everything needed to answer the questions in the Queries section. Build this in Google Sheets first (free, easy filtering) — graduate to Airtable or Postgres if your data grows beyond ~5,000 rows.

8 Queries That Actually Matter
These are the questions a database can answer that anecdotal review cannot. Run all 8 every Friday during your weekly review. The most important is the first — Setup Specialization Validation — because it directly drives your Phase 2 Months 6-7 picks.
🔄
Implementation Workflow
Setup once, log daily, review weekly, specialize in Months 6-7. The total time investment is roughly 5-10 minutes per day plus 30 minutes on Friday. The payoff is data-driven setup specialization that beats gut-feel selection 9 times out of 10.
Connection to Phase 2 Gate Criteria
Phase 2 Gate Criterion 2 requires positive expectancy on TWO setups across 15+ trades each. Your pattern database is what makes that criterion provable. Without it, you are guessing which setups work for you. With it, the data tells you — and the mentor verifies it during the exit interview.
